# AirGuard

AirGuard App allows you to get Air Quality data based on your location. 
- Current Air Quality including Air Quality Index, SO2, PM2.5, PM10, and more. 
- Interactive Map to see Air Pollution Levels anywhere in the world.
- Statistical Data for every parameter from November 2020. 
- Air Quality Forecast for 4 days in advance.
